Brief History

The State of Palestine has a long and complex history dating back to ancient civilizations. It has been a cradle of cultures and religions for thousands of years.

In the 20th century, Palestine became a central issue in the Arab-Israeli conflict. The 1948 Nakba resulted in the displacement of hundreds of thousands of Palestinians.

Today, Palestine is recognized by many countries and international organizations as a state under occupation, and its people continue to strive for sovereignty and peace.
